ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm AO Light. I'm a multi-media artist who works with fabric textiles, graphic work, painting, and sculpture. The "AO" stands for "artist of", as I consider myself an artist who uses light to tell a story. I've always had a love for intense and colorful works of art, and I want to translate that love into everything I make. Whether it's clothing, painting, or even digital work, I aspire to make bold pieces of art.
